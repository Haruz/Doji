In Our Opinion

"Give every child an equal opportunity in their life!"

All children have the right to education and upbringing, regardless of their nationality, place of birth, religion and race. They should be provided with safe, secure, easily affordable and motivational environments.

We want to provide local children and those able to access the facilities; with free, safe, social and educationally motivated environments. The place should be equipped to provide conducive environments for physical, educational, and social activities.

  The Premises

There are more than 12 kanals (65340 sq. feet) area which has ample space for physical and sports activities. There are also approximately 1000 sq. feet covered area which housing excellent facilities for various educational activities.

Ideal location

LET's school is located approximately 500 yards from the outskirts of Feroza city. Students may enjoy the benefits of both rural and urban areas whilst being able to access the school from two main roads. Students using the facilities at night will be able to use main Rahim Yar Khan road safely and securely.

Modern Learning Environments

Students to be provided with modern learning technologies having fast internet and printing facilities. All classrooms, and rooms for extracurricular activities will be air-conditioned. Students with mild learning and physical disabilities are catered for as well.
