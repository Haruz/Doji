Our Vision

Our vision is simple: empower the future generation of women by providing them with proficiency in English language and advance computer skills with a scope to contribute to Pakistan's social and economic successes. This will help them to become predominant members of the society and to support themselves and lead independent lives. We plan to target the most rural and impovershed areas and to establish schools that are more than simply training centres. LET believes in the future of Pakistan, where educated women stand side by side with men, working towards a better Pakistan and society. We aspire to be a nominally sustainable and academically proficient school, free for thosewho are at an economic disadvantage. We think, "Knowledge is power. Information is liberating. Education is the premise of progress, in every society, in every family".

  Our aims

We specifically aim to succeed in the following areas:

  • Providing teaching and learning experience of highest standard.
  • Providing motivating, interesting, engaging, and highly beneficial extracurricular actilvities.
  • Providing safe, secure, healthy, and free of any sort of coercion to allow students to flourish.
  • By creating and using our own resources and actively seeking to collaborate with others.
  • We will actively endeavor to provide educational facilities of international standards to our students from diverse background.
  • We aim to help local people, however, we will grow to be able to help people from all over the world.
